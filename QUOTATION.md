## 1. Project Summary

Development of a professional, responsive, multi-page website for **Niche Healthcare Group** — a healthcare services company operating across Australia and Zambia. The website will serve as the company's primary digital presence, showcasing its services (medical supply exports, dialysis services, healthcare consultancy, and clinical training), company information, and providing a functional contact/inquiry system.

### Phase 1 — Frontend Design & UI Implementation ✅ *(Completed)*

The visual design and frontend interface have already been developed and approved. This includes:

| # | Deliverable | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1.1 | **Home Page** — Hero section, capabilities overview, mission statement, and call-to-action sections | ✅ Complete |

### Phase 2 — Full Implementation & Deployment *(Requires 50% Payment to Begin)*

| # | Deliverable | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 2.1 | **Contact Form Backend** | Wire the contact/inquiry form to a working email delivery service (e.g., EmailJS, Formspree, or custom SMTP) so that submissions are sent directly to the client's inbox. Includes form validation and success/error feedback to the user. |
| 2.2 | **Newsletter Subscription** | Integrate the newsletter signup form with an email marketing platform or simple collection backend so subscriber emails are captured and stored. |
| 2.3 | **SEO Optimization** | Add proper meta tags (title, description, Open Graph, Twitter cards) to every page, semantic HTML5 improvements, structured data (JSON-LD for Organization), and sitemap generation for search engine indexing. |
| 2.4 | **Performance Optimization** | Image optimization (compression, lazy loading, WebP format), code splitting, asset minification, and Lighthouse audit to ensure fast load times. |
| 2.5 | **Browser Router Migration** | Migrate from HashRouter (`/#/about`) to BrowserRouter (`/about`) for clean, SEO-friendly URLs with proper server-side fallback configuration. |
| 2.6 | **Domain & Hosting Setup** | Assist with domain configuration and deploy the production build to a hosting platform (e.g., Vercel, Netlify, or client-preferred provider). Includes SSL certificate setup for HTTPS. |
| 2.7 | **Analytics Integration** | Set up Google Analytics (GA4) or equivalent tracking to monitor visitor traffic, page views, and user engagement metrics. |
| 2.8 | **Responsive QA & Cross-Browser Testing** | Thorough testing across devices (mobile, tablet, desktop) and browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) to ensure pixel-perfect rendering and full interactivity. |
| 2.9 | **Content Finalization** | Replace any placeholder content or stock images with client-provided assets. Final copy review and integration. |
| 2.10 | **Handover & Documentation** | Provide a simple maintenance guide explaining how to update content, redeploy, and manage the hosting environment. |
